This highly versatile sofa creates more space in your home. By serving many functions, it eliminates the need for a lot of your furniture.

The sofa also converts into an Ottoman, memory foam bed, bamboo table, Chaise lounge, and whichever configuration you like.

Because of it's modularity, you can change your living room in seconds. This makes your space more personal and reflects your mood.

Shape your living room to make you, or your guests, feel truly welcome. You can now host any event right at home.

You?

Julian Jorgensen

Born and raised in Denmark in the small town where LEGO first started. His parents built LEGO sculptures for living.

Starting at age 13, Julian was involved in numerous startups; from fashion clothing to electronics. He has contracted for many marketing agencies building corporate websites (with clients such as Adobe Inc. and real estate developers).

He travelled through Asia many years, living in Himalayas, Japan and Thailand. Influenced by Zen thinking, he found his true passion; creating more space in homes.

Julians goal in business is, to merge furniture and technology. From design to ordering - the furniture industry could face a new revolution.

We've used eco-friendly products such as Bamboo wood, bamboo foam and Ramie fibre. These materials are not only sustainable, but also have superior properties of their synthetic/non-sustainable siblings.
